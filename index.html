About the
Foundation

The Olanike Omopariola Foundation is a nonprofit dedicated to empowering lives through education, advocacy, and compassion. From grassroots outreach to national campaigns, we’re bridging gaps and restoring hope.

Our Mission & Focus Areas

Creating safe spaces and support for survivors.

Education Access

Hope in Her Hands

Fatima’s world was limited to the four walls of her home.She had never held a textbook—until the Foundation’s outreach program introduced her to literacy at age 13. Now, she reads bedtime stories to her siblings and dreams of becoming a teacher. “They didn’t just show up. They stayed. They taught me hope.”
