About the
Foundation

The Olanike Omopariola Foundation (TOOF) is a non-profit organization dedicated to ending domestic and gender-based violence and transforming the lives of women and children impacted by these injustices. We believe that every woman and child deserves to live free from fear, violence, and oppression.

Through bold advocacy, education, mentorship, and empowerment programs, TOOF works to break the cycle of abuse and poverty. We amplify survivor voices, challenge harmful norms, and influence policies that protect women and children. Our mission is clear: to create safe spaces, build resilience, and restore hope for a future where violence has no place.
